# Vacuum Cleaner Troubleshooting Guide

## Problem: Loss of Suction

### Possible Causes

* Full dust container
* Clogged hose
* Dirty filters

### Troubleshooting Steps

1. Empty dust container.
2. Inspect hose for blockages.
3. Clean or replace filters.

### Recommended Solution

Restore airflow by removing obstructions.

---

## Problem: Vacuum Will Not Start

### Possible Causes

* Power cord damage
* Faulty switch
* Overheated motor

### Troubleshooting Steps

1. Verify power outlet operation.
2. Inspect cord condition.
3. Allow motor to cool.

### Recommended Solution

Replace damaged electrical components.

---

## Problem: Burning Smell

### Possible Causes

* Blocked airflow
* Motor overheating
* Belt wear

### Troubleshooting Steps

1. Turn off vacuum immediately.
2. Inspect airflow path.
3. Check belt condition.

### Recommended Solution

Remove blockages and replace worn belts.
