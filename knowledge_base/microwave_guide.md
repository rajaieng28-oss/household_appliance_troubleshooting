# Microwave Troubleshooting Guide

## Problem: Microwave Not Heating

### Possible Causes

* Faulty magnetron
* Defective high-voltage diode
* Door switch malfunction

### Troubleshooting Steps

1. Verify power supply.
2. Check door latch operation.
3. Inspect internal components if qualified.

### Recommended Solution

Replace faulty magnetron or diode.

### Safety Precautions

* High-voltage capacitors can retain dangerous charge.
* Do not disassemble unless properly trained.

---

## Problem: Sparking Inside Microwave

### Possible Causes

* Metal objects inside
* Damaged waveguide cover
* Food residue buildup

### Troubleshooting Steps

1. Remove all metal objects.
2. Clean interior surfaces.
3. Inspect waveguide cover.

### Recommended Solution

Replace damaged waveguide cover and clean the interior.

---

## Problem: Turntable Not Rotating

### Possible Causes

* Damaged turntable motor
* Obstruction beneath tray

### Troubleshooting Steps

1. Remove and clean tray.
2. Inspect turntable motor.

### Recommended Solution

Replace faulty motor if required.
