# Air Conditioner Troubleshooting Guide

## Problem: AC Not Cooling

### Possible Causes

* Dirty air filter
* Low refrigerant
* Blocked condenser unit
* Thermostat issues

### Troubleshooting Steps

1. Clean or replace air filter.
2. Verify thermostat settings.
3. Inspect outdoor condenser unit.
4. Check airflow from vents.

### Recommended Solution

Replace dirty filters and clear airflow obstructions.

### Safety Precautions

* Disconnect power before servicing.

---

## Problem: Water Leakage

### Possible Causes

* Blocked condensate drain
* Frozen evaporator coil

### Troubleshooting Steps

1. Inspect drain line.
2. Remove blockages.
3. Allow frozen coils to thaw.

### Recommended Solution

Clear drain line and improve airflow.

---

## Problem: Strange Noises

### Possible Causes

* Loose fan blades
* Debris inside unit
* Motor wear

### Troubleshooting Steps

1. Inspect fan assembly.
2. Remove debris.
3. Check motor condition.

### Recommended Solution

Tighten components and replace worn parts.
